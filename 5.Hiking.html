The alluring wilderness of Sri Lanka, with its wide variety of both fauna and flora is irresistable to any avid hiker. Hiking or trekking is a marvelous way to explore what this already widely sought-after holiday destination has to offer. The dense rainforests and jungles that dot both the interior and coastline of the country store wonderful suprises in the forms of wildlife, waterfalls and breathtaking views. Don't forget to take your bug spray or wear boots when hiking in lush regions because those pesky mosquitos are something you will have to look out for.

Check out these amazing places if you are planning to go on a hike:

  1. Adam's Peak
  2. Knuckles Range
  3. Ella Rock
  4. Kitulgala Rainforest
  5. Meemure
