Your itinery wouldn't be complete if you don't experience the amazing safaris Sri Lanka has to offer! It is quite easily one of the best things to do in Sri :anla and when it comes to deciding where you want to go on safari, this little island does in fact have a wide range of options. There are twenty-two government protected national wildlife parks in Sri Lanka, where you can see animals such as bears, elephants, leopards and much more. The largest and most popular sites for safari rides are Yala (located around six hours from Colombo, well established resorts and inns), Wilpattu National Park (Oldest and largest in Sri Lanka) and Udawalawe National Park, famous for having a number of elephants so high that you are almost guaranteed to spot one.

Check out these other popular national parks which offer safari rides:

  1. Minneriya National Park
  2. Unawatuna National Park
  3. Sinharaja Forest Reserve
  4. Gal Oya National Park
  5. Wasgamuwa National Park
