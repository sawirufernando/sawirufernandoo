One of the fastest growing toursim industries is whale watching, and Sri Lanka is rapidly gaining fame in the whale-watching world for the opportunities to observe the massive blue whales in at least two different times of the year between December and July. While blue whales are no doubt the most sought after target of tours, you will also get an opportunity to see sperm whales and bottle-nosed dolphins too! whale watching tours sometimes head up to twenty kilometres offshore, so the vessels are usually large and powerful, even the Sri Lanka Navy offers whale watching tours.

These are the top places if you're looking to spot some blue ocean giants:

  1. Mirissa (south-west coast)
  2. Trincomalee (north-east coast)
  3. Kalpitiya (north-west coast)
