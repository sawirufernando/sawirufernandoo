Arugam Bay

Point Break, a famed tide spot which many regard as the best surf spot in Sri Lanka is situated in Arugam Bay. It's a place of modest size, home to not more than a few hundred and consisting of a single road which paralles the coastline. Beachfront guesthouses, oceanside restaurants dot this beautiful beachside which has a laidback and relaxed atmosphere which many surfers love. This crescent moon shaped beach is also a top attraction site for sun bathing and beach-volleyballl.

If you're looking for a place to stay near Arugam Bay, check out Whiskey Point Resort. Amazing hospitality and acommodation, just what you need to have the best surfing experience.

Nilaveli Beach

Located just a few kilomtres away from Trincomalee city, Nilaveli beach is a perfect spot for anyone wanting a relaxed beachside experience and stay in Sri Lanka. Pearly white sand, crystal clear water make this beach both a vibrant tourist and local attraction. A perfect spot for snorkelling, Pigeon Island is only a ten miunte boat ride away from the coast of Nilaveli. Fort Fredrick, Koneshwaram Temple and Gokanna Raja Monastery are also some key tourist sites locatd just a few kilometres off Nilaveli and are definetly woth a visit.

If you're looking for a place to stay while enjoying your vacation snorkelling, diving and sunbathing in Nilaveli, don't forget to check out Nilaveli beach hotel, which has a reputation for delcious meals and excellent hospitality.

Kalkudah Beach

A The less-frequented neighbour of Pasikuda beach, Kalkudah beach is a 4km long strech of sand that is a vision of serenity. Unlike Pasikuda beach, Kalkudah beach is much quieter with not as many stalls. You'll notice palm trees lining the coast, occasional fishermen pulling their boats ashore and sometimes animals such as cows belonging to nearby locals wandering aroud. This remote and calm spot is perfect for couples who would like to enjoy each other's time and company without the noise and crowds.

Aki Villa, located a few miles off Pasikuda and Kalkudah beach is an affordable yet luxurious villa that will no doubt amplify your experience at these beaches. Swimming pool, fruit picking along with a wide range of rooms from 2 bed to 6 beds are just few of the facilities that this place boasts.
